Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how WFS&S collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Identity verification documents (government-issued ID)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Receipts and transaction documentation
  • Communication preferences and support requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and geographic location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Log data and error reports
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Third-Party Information

  • Identity verification results from Stripe Identity
  • Payment processing data from Stripe
  • Credit and fraud prevention information
  • Partner referral tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process payments, issue virtual cards, manage accounts
  • Identity Verification: Comply with KYC requirements and prevent fraud
  • Communication: Send transaction confirmations, account updates, support responses
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and tax reporting obligations
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Partner Program: Track referrals and calculate commission payments

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing and identity verification
  • Twilio: SMS notifications and communications
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure data storage and application hosting
  • Email Services: Transactional and support communications

Legal Requirements

  • Compliance with court orders and legal processes
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • Protection of our legal rights and interests
  • Prevention of fraud and illegal activities

Business Transfers

  • Merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
  • Bankruptcy or reorganization proceedings
  • Due diligence processes with prospective buyers

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard hosting and database security
  • Payment Security: PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • Incident Response: Procedures for security breach notification
  • Employee Training: Security awareness and data protection training

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods based on purpose:

  • Account Information: Duration of active account plus 7 years
  • Payment Records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Identity Verification: 5 years after account closure
  • Communication Logs: 3 years for support and legal purposes
  • Usage Analytics: 2 years in aggregated, anonymized form
  • Marketing Data: Until consent withdrawal or opt-out

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have important rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

  • Request copies of your personal information
  • Download your data in portable formats
  • Receive information about data processing activities

Correction and Updates

  • Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Correct errors in your account profile
  • Modify communication preferences

Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Restrict processing for specific purposes
  • Object to automated decision-making

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance, security, or legitimate business purposes even after deletion requests.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Functions: Authentication, security, basic site functionality
  • Performance: Analytics to understand site usage and improve performance
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and customization choices
  • Security: Fraud prevention and protection against malicious activity

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though some functionality may be limited if cookies are disabled.

8. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for:

  • Privacy practices of external websites
  • Content or services provided by third parties
  • Data collection by linked services
  • Security of external platforms

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure:

  • Adequate protection for international transfers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Contractual safeguards with service providers
  • Regular assessment of transfer mechanisms

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We:

  • Do not knowingly collect information from minors
  • Require age verification during account creation
  • Delete any inadvertently collected minor data
  • Encourage parental supervision of online activities

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data security incident, we will:

  • Investigate and contain the breach immediately
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours when required
  • Report to relevant authorities as legally mandated
  • Provide clear information about the incident impact
  • Offer assistance and protective measures
  • Implement additional safeguards to prevent recurrence

12. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our services or business practices
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Enhanced privacy protections
  • User feedback and suggestions

Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • In-app notifications for mobile users
  • 30-day advance notice for significant changes

Privacy Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, contact us:

Email: privacy@wfss.com

Support: support@wfss.com

We're committed to protecting your privacy and will respond to your inquiries promptly.
